Overview

Purpose

Provide a thrilling and safe experience through White water rafting for a fee

Who are the target customers? Families or individuals with a desire to experience thrill and have more life experiences. People with high income.
What do they want? Know prices and locations for rafting.
What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Prices, locations, promos, details about rafting.
What are their needs that aren't being met? Knowing who and where can they go to practice rafting at an accessible price.

Audience

Families, as well as people in search for a thrilling yet safe experience
